Accountancy, Business, and Management strand

Strand Discription

The ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management strand) a comprehensive and practical education for students interested in the world of finance, entrepreneurship, and management. Through a blend of accounting principles, business concepts, and management strategies, students develop a strong foundation in financial literacy, critical thinking, and decision-making skills. Our program emphasizes real-world applications and experiential learning, equipping students with the knowledge and abilities needed to succeed in today's dynamic business environment. With a focus on developing leadership qualities and entrepreneurial mindset, the ABM strand prepares students for diverse career pathways in accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and business management, empowering them to become future leaders and innovators in the global marketplace.
