Garments and Fashion Design

Strand Discription

The GFD (Garments and Fashion Design) strand provides specialized education in apparel design and production. Students learn about fashion trends, textile materials, garment construction, and pattern making. Through hands-on training and creative projects, they develop skills in sewing, draping, sketching, and fashion illustration. The program covers topics such as fashion history, garment fitting, and clothing merchandising. Graduates are prepared for careers as fashion designers, pattern makers, garment technologists, or fashion stylists in the dynamic fashion industry.
